Odin Recipes - Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Shred the cheese and sprinkle it over the sauce.
  6. Place the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
